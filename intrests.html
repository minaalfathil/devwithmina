Intrests & Hobbies

What I Love to Do

Tech & Coding

I like learning new programming languages and working on small projects to test out what I’ve learned. It’s not just about memorizing syntax — I enjoy figuring out how things work behind the scenes and using code to build something from scratch

Tennis

I enjoy playing tennis when I can. It’s a fun way to stay active and take a break from screens. After spending a lot of time on my laptop, playing tennis helps me reset — especially when I start feeling drained from too much screen time. It keeps me moving and gives me a real-world break that actually feels refreshing.

Hiking

I like going on hikes when the chance comes up, especially when I need a break from screens. Being outside and walking through nature helps clear my head. My longest hike so far was a 15km one to Wadi El-Karak

Other Things

I like listening to music — it helps me focus when I’m working and unwind when I’m not. I’m also into movies and shows, especially ones with well-written characters, or just fun to watch when I need a break. It’s a simple way to relax and disconnect for a bit.
